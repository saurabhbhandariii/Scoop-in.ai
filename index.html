How It Works: Our Verification Process

Video Submission & Ingestion

Users submit videos for verification or our AI flags suspicious content. Videos are then broken down into core components.

AI-Powered Analysis

Our intelligent engines meticulously analyze every aspect: audio, visual, and behavioral patterns, leveraging cutting-edge AI.

Community Validation & Governance

The Scoop-in community reviews AI findings, contributes insights, and participates in transparent governance, adding a crucial human layer.

Transparent Verdict

A clear, blockchain-verified verdict is issued, detailing the truthfulness of the content and the evidence supporting it.

The Power Behind Scoop-in: Our Core Technology Stack

NLP-Driven Speech-to-Text

Sophisticated Natural Language Processing (NLP) converts spoken words within videos into accurate, searchable text. This enables deep textual analysis, keyword extraction, and cross-referencing with verified data sources to detect discrepancies in narratives.

Blockchain: Transparency & Integrity

Our backend leverages blockchain technology to ensure unparalleled transparency and immutability. Every verification result, community vote, and data point is recorded on a distributed ledger, providing an auditable and tamper-proof history.

  • Backend Transparency: A public, verifiable record of all analyses and verdicts.
  • Community Incentive & Governance Layer: Smart contracts power token-based rewards for accurate contributions and enable decentralized decision-making on platform rules and dispute resolution.

Behavioral Data Intelligence Engine

This engine analyzes patterns of dissemination, viewer engagement, and source behavior across various platforms. It identifies anomalous activities, coordinated disinformation campaigns, and potential deepfake indicators by mapping digital footprints and propagation strategies.

AI-Driven Anomaly Detection & Cross-Referencing

Our advanced AI continuously learns from new data, identifying subtle patterns indicative of deception. It cross-references claims and visual evidence against vast databases of facts, news archives, and known deepfake signatures, providing real-time alerts and flagging emerging threats.

Human Expert Oversight & Validation

While AI is powerful, critical verification cases are escalated to a network of human experts and fact-checkers. This synergistic approach ensures the highest level of accuracy, blending computational efficiency with nuanced human judgment.
