How Scoop-in.ai Works

Detectify blends real-time AI, blockchain, and community validation to verify mobile-first content at the speed of virality..

1

AI Fact-Check Engine

Continuously analyzes short-form videos using speech-to-text and NLP to extract factual claims.
Claims are matched against live knowledge graphs (e.g. Wikipedia, Wikidata) to detect misinformation instantly..

2

Behavioral Intelligence Engine

Tracks how users interact with fact-checks—measuring trust, skepticism, polarization, and content virality.
Builds a dynamic “Misinformation Behavior Graph” to power smarter, ethical content analysis.

3

Component Intelligence Agent

Maps real-world smartphone hardware by analyzing device fingerprints and user-submitted data.
Provides insights for semiconductor, display, and sensor companies seeking ground-truth market visibility.

4

Blockchain & Community Validation Layer

Verified claims and metadata are immutably recorded on blockchain (Polygon/Arweave).
A reputation-based system incentivizes users to flag, validate, and contribute to misinformation detection at scale.
