How Scoop-in Works

To combat misinformation and provide device intelligence, Scoop-in integrates AI-driven automation with human expertise.

1

Real-Time Fact Checking

Extracts audio from videos using speech-to-text technology. NLP pipeline identifies factual claims and verifies them against knowledge graphs. Results displayed in real-time via browser extension and mobile app.

2

Blockchain Transparency

Every verified claim and its metadata are immutably recorded on blockchain (Polygon, Arweave). Ensures public transparency, tamper resistance, and auditability for all fact-checking results.

3

Community Governance

Users flag, validate, and refute claims. Reputation system rewards accurate contributors. Token-based incentives drive community participation and data contribution.

4

Component Intelligence

Collects phone model data via user input and technical fingerprinting. Model-to-component mapping enables construction of real-world component penetration dataset valuable to semiconductor companies and investors.
